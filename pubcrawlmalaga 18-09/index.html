DESCRIPTION


Malaga Pub Crawl – After visiting Malaga at the day time, we will be continuing for the evening with the best pub crawl in town! Starting at our meeting point at Midnight at the New Bar Picasso at Plaza de la Merced, we will then take you on a tour showing you the city centre’s vibrant nightlife, on a circular walking/crawling route to make sure we end near where we start. Also We change bars and clubs every night, so you can be sure you can join more than one time!
